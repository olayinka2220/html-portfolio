The Best Movies According to Oluwatosin

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Needle in a Timestack

I love how the movie weaves together time travel and romance.

About: A sci-fi romance film that intricately blends time travel with love, exploring how the bonds of relationships endure across shifting timelines and altered realities.

Queen of Katwe

An inspiring tale of resilience, strategy, and triumph.

About: A young girl's journey from poverty to purpose, rising to greatness through strategic chess play.

Its Okay to Not be Okay

A poignant drama of healing, love, and emotional growth.

About: Korean drama about two wounded souls who find healing and love as they confront their pasts and emotional scars together.
